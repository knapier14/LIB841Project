Explore Kentucky for Educational Enrichment
Connect 		Mammoth Cave
As one of the worlds longest kown cave systems, this national park is sure to bring excitement through exploration and discovery. There is an abundance of opportunities for fun educational enrichment through cave tours, camping, hiking, canoeing, kayaking, boating, bicycling, stargazing, fishing, horseback riding, and so much more. Not only does this Kentucky gem provide hands on learning in science, but you can also explore the history and culture of the park. Visit the Mammoth Cave National Park Website to learn more, see upcoming events, contact the park, or begin making your visiting plans.
